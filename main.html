About Us

The Manchester Middle Eastern Indian Society (MMEIS) is a vibrant community that celebrates the rich cultures and traditions of the Middle East and India. We aim to bring together people from diverse backgrounds who share an interest in Middle Eastern and Indian cultures.

Events

Join us for a variety of events that showcase the best of Middle Eastern and Indian culture:

  • Cultural Nights
  • Food Festivals
  • Dance Workshops
  • Language Exchange Sessions
  • Music Performances
  • Movie Nights
  • Community Dinners
  • And much more!
